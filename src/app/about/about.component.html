About Page

Music is something that is loved all around the world, and has the ability to bring people from all different kinds of backgrounds together to create a sense of community. This is what we wanted to emphasize when making Feedback, that unity, to create an application that would connect people with their friends through music. The exposure of new music through Feedback can bring one closer to those they care about by bonding over their common music tastes, or can even encourage people to step out of their music comfort zones. The diversification of ones music taste can cause them to develop a more open minded view towards more than just music, which can even cause a person to become a better and more accepting person as they go throughout their life.
